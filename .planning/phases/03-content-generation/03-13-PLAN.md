<objective>
Close LEARN-05 gap: Add ad-hoc post support with theme balance exclusion.

The wizard currently has no concept of ad-hoc posts. Step4RenderReview always passes pillar, theme, and mechanic to updateBalance. Per LEARN-05, ad-hoc posts should be flagged and excluded from theme (and mechanic) balance calculation while still included in pillar balance. This requires: store field, UI toggle, conditional balance update, and ad_hoc flag on the post insert.

Purpose: Prevents one-off posts from skewing the theme/mechanic rotation balance while still tracking pillar distribution.
Output: Full-stack ad-hoc support from store to UI to IPC.
</objective>
